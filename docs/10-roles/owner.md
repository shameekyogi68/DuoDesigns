# 👑 Owner (Admin) Responsibilities

The Owner/Admin is the core orchestrator of the Duo Designs business and platform.

## 🎯 Primary Goal
To ensure every order is manufactured to high standards, dispatched within 48 hours, and that the platform catalog stays fresh and active.

---

## 📅 Daily Workflow

### 1. Order Management
- **8:00 AM:** Review all new `placed` orders.
- **9:00 AM:** Download customer designs and move orders to `confirmed`.
- **EOD:** Update tracking IDs for dispatched packages.

### 2. Inventory Check
- Review "Low Stock" signals on the dashboard.
- Contact suppliers to restock base apparel/accessories.
- Update digital stock counts once physical inventory arrives.

### 3. Financial Oversight
- Monitor daily sales trends via Dashboard charts.
- Pay out Partner commissions every Friday.
- Verify Razorpay payouts reach the company bank account.

---

## 🛠️ Authority Limits
- **Price Management:** Only the Admin can update `basePrice` or `discountPrice`.
- **Coupons:** Admin is responsible for creating and expiring discount codes for marketing campaigns.
- **Service Areas:** Admin manages the `Pincode` list to enable/disable delivery to specific cities.

## 🛡️ Support Handle
The Admin acts as the final decision maker for:
- Damage claims and refund requests.
- Technical issues (escalating to developers).
- Custom bulk order inquiries.
