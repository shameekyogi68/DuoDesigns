# 🎧 Support Agent Responsibilities

The Support Agent is the voice of Duo Designs, handling direct customer interactions and query resolution.

## 🎯 Primary Goal
To maintain a high Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) score by providing empathetic and fast resolutions.

## 📅 Key Tasks
1. **Inbox Triage:** Sorting `support@` emails into Priority vs. General info.
2. **Order Tracking:** Assisting customers who haven't received their dispatch email.
3. **Address Corrections:** Updating shipping details for orders *before* they are dispatched.
4. **Issue Documentation:** Creating clear bug reports for the engineering team when patterns emerge (e.g., "Mugs category images not loading on iOS").

## 🛡️ Empowerment
- Authorized to issue ₹100 discount coupons as goodwill for shipping delays.
- Authorized to escalate double-payment issues directly to the Founder for refund verification.
