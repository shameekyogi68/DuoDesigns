# Return & Cancellation Policy

Duo Designs values quality, but given the custom nature of our products, our policies are specific.

## 📦 Returns
- **Custom Designs**: Not eligible for return unless defective or damaged during transit.
- **Stock Items**: Eligible for return within **7 days** if unworn and in original packaging.
- **Process**: Customer must initiate return via the "Help" section in the app.

## ❌ Cancellations
- Orders can be cancelled until they reach the `dispatched` status.
- Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

# Privacy Policy (Summary)
We collect:
1. **PII**: Name, Email, Address for fulfillment.
2. **Metadata**: IP and browser info for security and analytics.
3. **Storage**: Your data is stored on MongoDB Atlas and encrypted in transit.

We **never** sell your data to 3rd party marketers.
