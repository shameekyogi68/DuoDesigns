# Marketing Agency Agreement (Duo Designs)

This document outlines the standard commercial terms between **Duo Designs** and its **Marketing Partners**.

## 🤝 The Partnership
The Partner is granted access to the **Revenue Tracker Portal** to promote Duo Designs products.

## 💰 Commission Structure
1.  **Standard Rate**: 10% of the taxable amount of all attributed sales.
2.  **Attribution**: Sales are attributed via referral code or partner-specific session tagging.
3.  **Payment Schedule**: 
    - Settlements are calculated monthly.
    - Payouts are transferred by the 10th of the following month.
    - Minimum payout threshold: ₹2,000.

## ⚖️ Responsibilities
- **Partner**: To market Duo Designs products in a professional manner consistent with brand guidelines.
- **Duo Designs**: To maintain high-quality fulfillment and accurate revenue tracking.
