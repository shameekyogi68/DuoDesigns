# 💰 Pricing Strategy

Detailed internal guide on how Duo Designs prices products and services.

## 1. Product Pricing Structure
- **Base Price:** Covers the cost of the blank garment/accessory + single-side print + basic labor.
- **XL/XXL Addon:** +₹50 (due to increased fabric consumption and weight).
- **Double-Sided Print:** +₹80 (extra ink + secondary setup time).

## 2. Shipping Charges
- **Standard:** ₹80 flat for orders below ₹999.
- **Free Shipping:** Automatic for orders ≥ ₹999 within India.
- **Priority (Express):** (Future) Fixed ₹150 for 24hr dispatch.

## 3. Discount & Coupon Logic
- **New User:** 10% Off (Code: `DUO10`) - One-time use.
- **Bulk (5+ items):** Automatic 15% discount applied at checkout.
- **Seasonal:** 20% max discount during festival sales.
- **Note:** Coupons cannot be stacked with "Sale Items".

## 4. Taxes (GST)
- **HSN 6109 (Apparel):** 5% GST (if < ₹1000) or 12% (if > ₹1000). *Current system defaults to standard 12% for simplicity.*
- **HSN 6912 (Ceramics):** 12%.
