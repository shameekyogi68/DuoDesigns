# ⚖️ Legal Compliance Content

This document contains the legally binding policy text required for Duo Designs under the **Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules 2020** in India.

## 1. Privacy Policy
- **Data Collection:** We collect Name, Email, Phone, and Address for order fulfillment and OTP authentication.
- **Data Usage:** Data is used solely for order processing and platform security. We do not sell data to third parties.
- **Cookies:** We use functional cookies for session management (JWT) and cart persistence.

## 2. Terms of Service
- **User Liability:** Users are responsible for the content of their uploaded designs.
- **Prohibited Content:** We reserve the right to reject designs containing hate speech, illegal symbols, or copyright infringement.
- **Pricing:** Prices are subject to change without notice but are locked once an order is placed.

## 3. Return & Refund Policy (Duo Designs Specific)
- **Custom Prints:** Non-returnable unless defective.
- **Damaged Goods:** Claims must be made via `support@duodesigns.in` with photos within 48 hours of delivery.
- **Timeline:** Refunds reflect in the original payment method within 7 business days.

## 4. Shipping & Delivery
- **Dispatch:** Orders processed within 48-72 recursive business hours.
- **Tracking:** Real-time tracking IDs provided via Email and WhatsApp.
- **Serviceability:** Delivery only to pincodes verified in our database.
