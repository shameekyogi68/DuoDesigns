# 📈 Business Metrics & KPIs

To scale Duo Designs effectively, we track these critical performance indicators.

## 1. Sales & Conversion
- **Conversion Rate (CR):** % of visitors who place an order.
- **Add-to-Cart Rate:** % of visitors who add at least one item.
- **Cart Abandonment Rate:** (Abandoned / Total Carts) x 100.
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** Total Revenue / Number of Orders.

## 2. Product & Design Insights
- **Most Popular Category:** (Oversized Tees vs. Mugs etc).
- **Design Type Split:** % of Uploaded Designs vs. % of Shop Premade Designs.
- **Refund Rate:** % of orders resulting in returns/refunds (Target: <2%).

## 3. Operational Performance
- **Fulfillment Time:** Average time from "Placed" to "Dispatched".
- **Delivery Time:** Average time from "Dispatched" to "Delivered".
- **Cost of Customer Acquisition (CAC):** Total Ad Spend / New Customers.

## 4. Geographical Data
- **Top Pincodes:** Identifying high-demand clusters in India.
- **State Tax Split:** Ratio of CGST+SGST vs. IGST orders.
