# 🚫 Cancellation & Refund Policy

Our policy is designed to protect both the customer and the manufacturing operations of Duo Designs.

## 1. Cancellation Rules
- **Before Confirmation:** Customers can cancel their order instantly from "My Account" if the status is `placed`.
- **After Confirmation:** Once an order is `confirmed` (meaning it has entered the production queue), cancellation is **not permitted**.
- **After Dispatch:** Dispatched items cannot be cancelled.

## 2. Refund Procedure
- **Automated:** Approved cancellations trigger an automatic refund to the original payment method via Razorpay.
- **Deductions:** No cancellation fees are applied for orders cancelled within the allowed window.
- **Timeline:** Funds typically reflect in 5-10 business days per Indian banking standards.

---

## 3. Damaged / Incorrect Items
In cases of manufacturing defects or wrong items delivered:
1. Customer must contact `support@duodesigns.in` with photos.
2. Admin reviews the case.
3. If approved, a full refund OR a free replacement is processed immediately.
