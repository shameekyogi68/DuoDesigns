# 📞 Customer Support Workflow

How we handle and resolve customer inquiries at Duo Designs.

## 1. Channels
- **Email:** support@duodesigns.in (Primary, 24-hr resolution goal).
- **WhatsApp:** Shared business number for quick tracking queries.
- **Help Center:** In-app FAQ and tracking portal.

## 2. Escalation Levels
| Level | Handled By | Typical Issue |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **L1** | Shared Inbox | Tracking queries, price info, basic login issues. |
| **L2** | Support Lead | Failed payments, cancellation requests, address changes. |
| **L3** | Founder/Admin | Manufacturing defects, returns, legal/grievance claims. |

## 3. Standard Response Templates
- **Payment Verification:** "We have initiated verification with our bank. This takes 2-4 hours..."
- **Return Request:** "Please upload clear photos of the defect to start the L3 review..."
- **Tracking Delay:** "We apologize. Your package is currently with DTDC and delayed due to [reason]..."

## 4. Resolution SLA
- **Response:** Within 4 business hours.
- **Resolution:** Within 24-48 business hours.
