# Spacing & Brand Assets

## 📐 Spacing System (8pt Grid)
We follow an 8-pixel baseline for all margins, paddings, and layouts.

| Label | Value | Usage |
|-------|-------|-------|
| `base`| 8px | Border radius, small gaps. |
| `md`  | 16px | Card padding, form spacing. |
| `lg`  | 32px | Section vertical spacing. |
| `xl`  | 64px | Hero section margins. |

## 🏷️ Brand Assets

### Logo
- **Main**: Black on White (Primary).
- **Secondary**: White on Black (For footer/Hero).
- **Icon**: Simplified "D" emblem for favicons and loading spinners.

### Photography
- High contrast, editorial style.
- Desaturated or neutral backgrounds to let the products (Electric Lime, etc.) stand out.
