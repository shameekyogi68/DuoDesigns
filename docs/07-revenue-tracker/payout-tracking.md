# Payout Tracking

The **Monthly Settlements** page tracks the flow of funds from Duo Designs to the Agency.

## 💰 Payout Lifecycle

1.  **Draft**: Sales are currently accumulating for the month. Total updates in real-time.
2.  **Requested**: At month-end, the agency "requests" settlement for the final amount.
3.  **Approved**: Duo Designs admin has verified the sales and cleared the amount for transfer.
4.  **Paid**: Payment reference added. The month is marked "Closed".

## 📊 Monthly Invoicing
The agency can click **"Download Statement"** on any closed month to get a PDF breakdown of the sales that comprised that payout.
