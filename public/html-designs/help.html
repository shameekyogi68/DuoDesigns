SHIPPING POLICY

All orders are prepaid only. We do not offer Cash on Delivery.

Processing Time

All products at Duo Designs are custom-printed on demand. Once your order is placed and payment confirmed, your order enters our print queue within 1–2 hours.

Printing and packing typically takes 2–3 business days (Monday–Saturday, excluding public holidays).

Delivery Time

After dispatch, expected delivery times are:

  • Bengaluru city: 1–2 business days
  • Karnataka (other cities): 2–4 business days
  • South India: 3–5 business days
  • Rest of India: 4–7 business days

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are based on your delivery pincode and are displayed at checkout before payment.

Free shipping on all orders above ₹999 (automatically applied).

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an email with the courier partner name and tracking number. Use our Track Order page to check status anytime.

Delivery Delays

Delivery timelines may be affected during peak seasons, national holidays, or adverse weather conditions. We are not liable for courier delays once the shipment is handed over to the delivery partner.

REFUND & RETURN POLICY

⚠️ Since all products are custom-printed to order, we have a strict no-return policy except for defective or damaged items.

When Can You Claim a Refund?

We accept refund/replacement requests only in the following cases:

  • Wrong product delivered (different from what was ordered)
  • Manufacturing defect in the product
  • Print error — significantly different from approved design
  • Product damaged during shipping

How to Raise a Claim

Raise a request within 48 hours of delivery by emailing us at support@duodesigns.in with:

  • Your Order ID
  • Clear photos of the defective/damaged item
  • Brief description of the issue

Resolution

We will review your request within 2–3 business days and offer either a replacement or a full refund to your original payment method. Refunds typically reflect within 5–7 business days.

Non-Returnable Cases

  • Change of mind after order placement
  • Incorrect size chosen by customer (please use our size chart)
  • Slight colour variation due to screen differences
  • Claims raised after 48 hours of delivery
